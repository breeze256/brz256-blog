## 放缩

$$
\frac{1}{\e}\e^{\frac{1}{\e}x} \geq \ln x, \quad \mathrm{iff} \ x=\e 
$$

![fig](/images/screenshot_2026-02-21_18-31-26.png)

## 证明

$$
\begin{gather}
\mathrm{let} \ f(x)= \frac{1}{\e}\e^{\frac{1}{\e}x} - \ln x = \e^{\frac{1}{\e}x-1} - \ln x\\
f'(x) = \e^{\frac{1}{\e}x-2} -\frac{1}{x}
\end{gather}
$$
注意到，$f(x)$为单峰函数且$x = \e$是$f(x)$的唯一极小值点，又

$$
f(\e) = \frac{1}{\e}\e^{\frac{1}{\e}\e} - \ln \e = 1-1 = 0
$$

故$\dfrac{1}{\e}\e^{\frac{1}{\e}x} \geq \ln x$，且两侧相切于$(\e,1)$，证毕。
