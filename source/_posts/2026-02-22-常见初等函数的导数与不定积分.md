## 幂、指、对函数

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\frac{\d}{\d x}x^\alpha = \alpha x^{\alpha -1} \qquad \qquad \qquad&
&\int x^\alpha \d x = \frac{1}{\alpha+1}x^{\alpha+1} \\
&\frac{\d}{\d x}a^x = a^x\ln a & &\int a^x\d x = \frac{a^x}{\ln a} \\
&\frac{\d}{\d x} \log_a x = \frac{1}{x\ln a} & &\int \log_ax \d x =\frac{x\ln x - x}{\ln a} \\
\end{aligned}
$$
